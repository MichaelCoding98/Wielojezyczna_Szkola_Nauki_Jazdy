1.The "ALP" Driver Training Center conducts training for candidates at B, B1, B-Automat drivers, professional qualifications.

2. The "ALP" Driver Training Center is obliged to carry out theoretical and practical classes in a specific place and time in the quantity set in the course price and not less than the statutory amount. Hour theoretical lessons are 45 minutes and practical 60 minutes. Activities theoretical knowledge can be done using e-learning.

3. The training ends with an internal theoretical exam and practical carried out in the presence of an instructor. Exam theoretical internal is not performed for the courses for which it is not required by law.

4. The positive result of the internal practical exam is unequivocal with the issuance of a certificate of completion of the course and updating the profile candidate for the driver of the "PKK". The cost of the exam is included in the course fee and takes place after all practical classes have been completed.

5. The internal examination may be taken multiple times, except Theoretical make-up exam is free, while the exam is free of charge practical internal is payable. Re-sit exam fee practical internal is equivalent to 1 additional hour of training practical for a given driving license category.

6. The center provides the opportunity to do medical examinations, psychological and the availability of training materials, classes with pre-medical first aid, with qualified rescuers medical.

7. The trainee has the right to free consultations with the Driving instructor, the right to use a computer room along with the necessary one software needed for learning throughout the training period including the waiting time for the state examination.

8. A candidate for a driver, also known as a trainee, is obliged to systematic participation in theoretical and practical classes. IN the case of classes with the use of e-learning techniques, the participant of the training may take theoretical classes outside OSK, which does not exempt the participant from participation in exercises and classes in first aid pre-medical.

9. If it is not possible to participate in the classes, the student is obliged to notify about the absence of the leading instructor and the manager of the center at least 48 hours before class, and in case of failure to notify the payment of the fee for each agreed driving hour according to the resort's pricelist.

10. The student is obliged to notify the center manager of any training problems, with the center manager for consideration urgent claims and take appropriate steps.

11. In justified cases, there is a possibility of random situations withdrawal from the fee contained in point 9 by written request to center manager.

12. The student is obliged to make systematic payments so as to finish the course was fully paid.

13. The student has the right to withdraw from the contract within the statutory period of 14 days. Withdrawal from the contract may take place on the basis of the submitted a legible receipt or invoice for the training purchase. Withdrawal from the contract does not release the student from fees for medical and psychological examinations, training materials and completed rides. Discontinuation of training no exempts the student from paying the training fee. Discontinuation of training occurs at the written request of the trainee. In the event of an interruption training, OSK may waive a portion of the fee depending on the number hours of training used. The minimum fee is 30% of the training price.

In matters not covered by these regulations, the provisions of the Civil Code shall apply.
